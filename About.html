About CHAMBO Scout Troop

CHAMBO Scout Troop Malawi is the country’s first officially registered Troop to pioneer a digital-first Scouting approach. Based in Lilongwe and operating under the Scouts Association of Malawi (SAM), we blend Scouting tradition with modern innovation to prepare young people for leadership, service, and sustainable impact.

Our Vision

To raise Scouts who are grounded in Scouting values and equipped with practical and digital skills ready to serve their communities, lead with integrity, and shape Malawi’s future.

Our Mission

To empower youth through discipline, teamwork, digital literacy, and life skills. CHAMBO supports the full Scouting journey from Cub Scouts to Troop Leaders ensuring every Scout grows in both tradition and technology.

Our Goals & Aspirations

• Youth Resource Center: A space for skills training, mentorship, and empowerment offering trades like carpentry, welding, farming, and craftsmanship.
• Innovation & Digital Growth: Teaching coding, robotics, and creative digital skills including Malawi’s first Online Scout Badges.
• Leadership Development: Guiding Scouts from their first badge to leadership roles, preparing them for real-world challenges and community impact.

CHAMBO Troop Projects

Digital Scout Malawi

CHAMBO’s first digital first initiative, connecting Scouts across Malawi through coding, online collaboration, and digital leadership. This Troop led project ensures every Scout from Chitipa to Nsanje can access opportunities, recognition, and community through technology.

CleanGuard Hygiene Patrols

CHAMBO’s second major initiative, empowering Scouts to lead hygiene campaigns, water safety education, and outbreak prevention. From handwashing stations to clean water mapping, our patrols protect lives while living the Promise.
