About CHAMBO Scout Troop

Chambo Scout Troop Malawi is the nation’s first Troop, digital-first Scouting initiative — officially recognized by the Scout Association of Malawi. Headquartered in Lilongwe, we combine Scouting traditions with digital innovation to prepare young people for the challenges of today and tomorrow.

Our Vision

To develop Scouts who are rooted in Scouting values yet equipped with modern digital and practical skills — ready to serve, innovate, and lead Malawi into a brighter future.

Our Mission

To empower young people with discipline, teamwork, digital literacy, and life skills. We aim to cover the full Scouting journey — from Cub Scouts to Leaders — ensuring that no one is left behind in the digital world and traditional Scouting.

Our Goals & Aspirations

• Youth Resource Center : A hub for skills training, counseling, and empowerment — offering trades such as carpentry, welding, plumbing, farming, and craftsmanship.
• Innovation & Digital Growth: Teaching coding, robotics, and creative digital skills, including Malawi’s first Online Scout Badges.
• Leadership Development: Supporting Scouts from their first Cub badge to leadership roles, preparing them for real life and sustainable livelihoods.

Why the Name CHAMBO?

We chose the name CHAMBO because the Chambo fish is a proud symbol of Malawi — a source of food, resilience, and unity for our communities. Just as the Chambo nourishes families, our troop nourishes the spirit of Scouting and leadership. We also draw inspiration from Jepeph Peter Kamvabingu, representing wisdom and cultural heritage — reminding us to stay rooted in tradition while embracing innovation.

Our First Project: Digital Scout Malawi

Digital Scout Malawi is our pioneer initiative to connect Scouts across the nation through digital platforms. It promotes coding, online collaboration, and digital leadership while ensuring every Scout — from Chitipa to Nsanje — can access opportunities and recognition online.
