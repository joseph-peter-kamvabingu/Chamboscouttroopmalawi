CHAMBO & the SDGs

CHAMBO Scout Troop Malawi is driving youth-led action across the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). From clean water patrols to digital literacy campaigns, our Scouts are turning global goals into local impact.

We focus on SDG 6 (Clean Water & Sanitation), SDG 13 (Climate Action), SDG 4 (Quality Education), and SDG 17 (Partnerships for the Goals). Our patrols map water sources, teach sanitation, promote digital skills, and build bridges with changemakers across Malawi and beyond.

Whether you're a Scout troop, youth organization, school, or NGO — we invite you to collaborate on SDG-aligned projects. Your support helps us scale our impact, empower more youth, and preserve both tradition and innovation.
