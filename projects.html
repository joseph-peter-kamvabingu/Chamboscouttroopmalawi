CHAMBO SCOUT TROOP MALAWI & the SDGs

We are CHAMBO Scout Troop Malawi young leaders taking real action on the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). From clean water patrols to digital literacy missions, we’re turning global goals into local impact, one project at a time.

Our focus is clear: SDG 6 (Clean Water & Sanitation), SDG 13 (Climate Action), SDG 4 (Quality Education), and SDG 17 (Partnerships for the Goals). We map water sources, teach hygiene, run coding workshops, and connect with changemakers across Malawi and beyond all led by Scouts, not offices.

If you're a fellow Scout troop, youth group, school, or NGO we’d love to team up. Your support helps us grow our patrols, reach more communities, and keep blending tradition with innovation. Let’s build something powerful together.
