CHAMBO Scout Badges

CHAMBO badges are more than symbols—they're milestones of growth, service, and innovation. Each badge is designed by Scouts, for Scouts, reflecting our journey from Cub to Leader, from bushcraft to robotics, from tradition to transformation.

Camping & Bushcraft Badge

Earned by Scouts who master outdoor living, tent setup, and survival skills in nature—rooted in Scouting tradition.

Fire-Making & Survival Badge

Awarded for safe fire-building, emergency readiness, and wilderness resilience—living the Promise under pressure.

Knot Tying & Pioneering Badge

Recognizes Scouts who build with skill—lashings, bridges, and structures that serve and inspire.

Cultural Storytelling Badge

For Scouts who preserve oral traditions, songs, and legends—keeping Malawi’s spirit alive through voice and memory.

Chichewa Language Badge

Earned by Scouts who learn, teach, or translate in Chichewa—promoting inclusion, pride, and national identity.

Handcraft & Artisan Badge

Awarded for creating local crafts—beadwork, weaving, pottery, and design rooted in heritage and skill.

Robotics Explorer Badge

For Scouts who build or program basic robots—exploring automation, creativity, and future-ready thinking.

Coding & Web Design Badge

Awarded for creating websites, apps, or digital tools that solve real problems—Scout-led innovation in action.

AI & Emerging Tech Badge

For Scouts who explore artificial intelligence, data, and future technologies—leading Malawi into tomorrow.

Cub Scout Starter Badge

First badge for new Scouts who complete orientation, learn the Law, and begin their journey with pride.

Patrol Leader Badge

For Scouts who lead small teams, organize troop activities, and model servant leadership in action.

Global Exchange Badge

Earned by Scouts who collaborate with international Troops, join global events, and build friendships across borders.

Beyond the Badge

CHAMBO Scout Badges carry legacy. They honor the hands that build, the voices that lead, and the minds that imagine. Whether you’re a Scout in Malawi or a friend across the globe, we invite you to exchange badge stories, co-create new ones, and celebrate the skills that unite us.

From traditional bushcraft to digital innovation, our badge system is Scout-led, Troop-designed, and always evolving. Join us in shaping the next chapter of Scouting—one badge, one story, one Scout at a time.
