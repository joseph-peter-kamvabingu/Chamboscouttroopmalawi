Our Programs

CHAMBO Scout Troop Malawi is the first Scout troop in the country to blend traditional Scouting with a digital-first approach, proudly registered under the Scouts Association of Malawi (SAM).

We uphold timeless Scouting values leadership, service, and ceremony while using digital tools to connect, create, and empower youth across Malawi and beyond.

We’ve launched Defutalks Badges to celebrate youth voices and digital storytelling, and we’re initiating a bold new project: Digital Scout Malawi a platform to showcase patrols, badges, and youth-led impact across the nation.

CHAMBO Scout Troop Launch

On 7th September 2025, CHAMBO Scout Troop Malawi was officially launched — blending tradition, ceremony, and digital innovation. Watch the moment we began shaping the future of Scouting in Malawi and beyond.
