CHAMBO Scout Troop Malawi

A new chapter in Scouting where tradition meets technology to empower young people, strengthen patrols, and take action for a better Malawi.

As a registered Troop under the Scout Association of Malawi, CHAMBO is led by a dedicated team of youth and mentors. Together, we champion skills for life, community service, and SDG‑driven projects building a legacy for the next generation of Scouts.

Our Programs

CHAMBO Scout Troop Malawi is the first registered Scout Troop in the country to pioneer a digital-first approach while honoring Scouting tradition. We operate proudly under the Scouts Association of Malawi (SAM).

We uphold timeless Scouting values leadership, service, and ceremony while using digital tools to connect, create, and empower youth across Malawi and beyond.

We’ve launched Defutalks Badges to celebrate youth voices and digital storytelling, and we’re initiating a bold new project: Digital Scout Malawi, a platform to showcase patrols, badges, and youth-led impact across the nation.

Digital Patrols

Scout-led patrols focused on leadership, clean water, climate action, and digital innovation guided by CHAMBO’s Troop values.

Partner With Chambo

Chambo Scout Troop Malawi welcomes collaboration with fellow Scout Troops, youth-led groups, and changemakers across the globe. Whether you're looking to co-host a training, exchange patrol stories, or build a joint SDG project we’re ready to connect as a proud Troop under Scout Association of Malawi SAM.

We also invite individuals and organizations who believe in youth empowerment to support our mission. Your donation helps us expand digital access for Scouts across Malawi while preserving the heart of Scouting ceremony, service, and leadership. Every contribution strengthens our Troop’s ability to innovate, honor tradition, and empower the next generation of Scouts to lead with pride and purpose.
